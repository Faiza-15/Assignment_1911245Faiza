About Tweety Bird

Tweety is a yellow canary in the Warner Bros. Looney Tunes and Merrie Melodies series of
animated cartoons.

The name "Tweety" is a play on words, as it originally meant "sweetie", along with "tweet"
being an English onomatopoeia for the sounds of birds.
